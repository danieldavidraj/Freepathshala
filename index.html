WHO WE ARE

Freepathshala is a Social initiative started in May 2015 working towards ensuring construction workers kids get continues education despite of their parents living nomadic life. We are a full time free school operating from public parks of gurgaon.

" We are a bridge school who teaches kids without any commitment of they being regular to school. once kids are regular to school we build stability in there parents life to ensure no future drop outs. "

WHAT WE DO

-Parent Teaching Meeting
This is the most important part of our initiative. Along with routinely tips on how to help kid excel in studies, we meet children’s parents every month to advice / counsel them on how they can come out of nomadic life routine.

-Free Education
Education is not always free. But we aspire to make it for those who can’t afford it. Around 2,00,000 construction workers depend on daily wages and hence, don’t get to send their children to school for regular classes. Teachers, volunteers, youth, and parents join us in organizing daily classes in the public parks of Gurgaon. We currently operate in sector 45,46,51,52,57.

-Health Checkup Camp
We organize regular health checkup camps in partnership with Doctors living in Gurgaon. Since construction workers work at a very polluted and in extremely unhygienic conditions every day, it becomes more important for them to care about their health than that for a privileged urban-living resident. The dust, smoke, sweat, and contaminants are consumed by them without a hint of care which leads to health-related issues and adds up to more problems in their lives.
