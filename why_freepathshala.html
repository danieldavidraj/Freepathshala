BRINGING SCHOOLS CLOSER TO CONSTRUCTION WORKERS KIDS

If you prefer not to see the Live Corners widget when you select the Direct Selection Tool,you can turn it off from View > Hide Corner Widget. If you want to continue seeing the Live Corners widget when you select the Direct Selection Tool ,click View > Show Corner Widget.

In most of the Govt schools fee is bare minimum and can be afforded anyone in particular. But it is for those who can afford to send their children to schools. Why others canâ€™t?

Construction workers working for a small construction sites have to keep changing their location every 2-3 months because every construction site need different skill set to work on after every 2-3 months. Which means their kids will have to walk alone to a new school location. Travelling alone for a 5-14yr old kid is neither safe nor advisable.

To resolve that situation, you need school which:

  • Is Located at convenient location from nearby construction site.
  • These schools should also have presence in multiple nearby locations because construction workers may get a job in adjoining sectors.
  • Such schools should have similar curriculum and preferable kids should be from similar section of society.
  • School should be open to take admission any time of year
  • School should not charge a fee because many uneducated parents will not like to spend money in text books every 2-3 months.

Freepathshala does it all and little bit more to reduce nomadic life / negative impact.
