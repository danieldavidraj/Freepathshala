Sammaan Ka Saman is an initiative to help construction laborers acquire their house hold items in a dignified manner.

Every house hold discards many items which get replaced by new one but can be reused. so itâ€™s an opportunity for your such discarded stuff to build a feeling of new to those for whom this could be a dream only.

We accept old kitchen items, adult cloths, bed sheets, curtains from societies and sell at a nominal amount ( Rs. 30 / 60 / 90 ) to construction laborers around us.

Collected amount is used to run freepathshala in a self sustainable manner. While the stuff for 5 to 15yrs old kids still get distributed free only.

If you wish to handover your old stuff for Reuse or wish to volunteer in liquidating the stuff please give us a shout.
